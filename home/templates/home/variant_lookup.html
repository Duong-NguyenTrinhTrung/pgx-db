VARIANTS

Users can search for variant data occurring on our collected genes. For each variant, we retrieved its basic annotation (gene, transcript, protein position, wildtype and mutant amino acid, codons), its association study statistics (sourced from Genebass ) and corresponding variant effect prediction scores (sourced from Ensembl ) from over 41 algorithms, including SIFT, Polyphen, PrimateAI, and AlphaMissense, normalized to facilitate comparisons.
